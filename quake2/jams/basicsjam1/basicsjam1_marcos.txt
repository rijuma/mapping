* Introduction *

  The Strogg are regrouping on Omicron base, they are preparing a new
  abominable machine that could hit us hard. Your mission is to infiltrate
  the Omicron base and destroy their secret weapon. Get to the Warehouse
  first to get some gear. Give them hell, soldier!
